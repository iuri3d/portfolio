THE DEATHLY HALLOWS

__________________

Illustration of "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows" book cover

















I started this illustration in 2009, when the book first came out. At the time, I was not comfortable about the colour pencil techniques, but it was because of that drawing that I’ve started to develop a passion for this style. It’s a work-in-progress drawing that I never finished because of an old habit, start a new drawing without finishing the last one I've been working in. However, I think that the sketch "melted" with the painted areas results in an interesting combination.














JON SNOW PORTRAIT

__________________

Quick Illustration of Jon Snow, one of the main characters of Game of Thrones

















In 2015, after my graduation, I decided to return to the old hobby that I’ve been pulling apart. To develop the speed that I’ve lost after four years without drawing, I thought that the best way to train it was choosing some characters from popular tv shows and doing a fast draw of it in less than thirty minutes. Game of Thrones is one of the most popular tv shows at the time, and Jon Snow one of the most popular characters, so I choose him for my first drawing. I just used three graffiti pencils and shading techniques to draw the shadows and the highlights.
