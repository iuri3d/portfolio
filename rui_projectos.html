Comic Flyer

__________________

The Comic Flyer application was the first project I developed for the tablet format. It is an application that brings first-hand audiovisual content and the latest news from the comicbook culture, from books to the big screen





Development

__________________

Comic flyer was born from the idea of searching for an application that establishes itself in a single space diverse contents from books, animated series, exclusive interviews to writers and publishers, among others. The project management has been developed in several bases and concepts to accommodate this type of projects















OBNINBUS

__________________

Obninbus is a teaser of a fictional video game that aimed to develop 3D modeling, character design, and story telling capabilities













The Story

__________________

In a distant galaxy, the system called Cellis, the planets Usion, Ratak, Proneum, Gemzar and Zarok joined cultures to cope with the times of oppression and instability caused by the fearsome Urakus. In a great alliance, a ship built and a crew formed, with one member from each planet, to enter the OBININBUS mission















Modeling

__________________

The modeling of the models was done in the programs of Maya from the autodesk and Keyshot from the luxion. Once with the conceptions of art made, the construction of the models starts from their skeleton to their texture











Character Design

__________________

Above we have the conception of the Crog character of the planet Zarok. He is a tall, yellow-eyed, reptilian character. It is a vengeful, protective, humble, and sometimes arrogant fighter. His wardrobe was inspired in the medieval armor and Marine soldiers










AURA

__________________

Aura is an exhibition proposal for MoMa that counts on a collaboration between the studio Untold Arts and visual artist Mathias Müller. Together in an exhibition that brings the abstraction of his aura in art

































Exhibition

__________________

In a superior area to 2000m², the visitor will be going to find projections of particles of creativity preserved in his most pure state. In a dark environment, the light of the projections will reflect on the pieces of the exposed hearts recreating an inner aura













94 Lost Cannon

__________________

94 Lost Cannon is a teaser trailer presenting a LEGO construction model performed by Untold Arts studio whose theme and design is inspired by Ridley Scott's film Alien: The Eighth Passenger. For this, the teaser uses a sense of mystery and science fiction
