ME

My name is Rui Fernandes and I am a designer, editor and multidisciplinary illustrator. I have a great passion for the cultural culture of science fiction and everything that is adjacent to it. I am also a big fan of LEGO pieces, and even today I build my stories in high complex models made entirely of LEGO. So if you did not already know that I'm huge geek at heart, you will after browsing through some of my work.
